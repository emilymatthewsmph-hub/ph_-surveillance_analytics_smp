# Limitations

This project is subject to the following limitations:

- All data are synthetic and do not reflect true disease patterns or seasonality.
- Syndrome definitions are simplified and do not capture full clinical nuance.
- No risk adjustment or demographic weighting is applied.
- Anomaly detection does not account for historical seasonal baselines.
- Laboratory confirmation data is not incorporated into surveillance logic.

These limitations are intentional and reflect a design choice to prioritize interpretability and reproducibility in a portfolio setting.
